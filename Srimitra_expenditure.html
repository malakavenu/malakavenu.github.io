Key Insights & Recommendations

Summary of findings from the 6-month expenditure analysis.

Stable Core Operations
Fogging (₹73.5K), BBMP Garbage (₹48K), and Manager Salary (₹69K) remained perfectly flat across both quarters — no cost creep. Recurring expenses grew only 3.8% QoQ when one-time items are excluded.
Water is 31.8% of All Spend
At ₹15.23L over 6 months (₹2.54L/month), water supply is the single largest cost. SAL Water Tanker alone is ₹9.50L. Exploring rainwater harvesting or bore well optimization could significantly reduce costs.
Generator Costs Tripled (+159%)
From ₹36.8K to ₹95.3K — driven by battery replacement (₹29K) and higher diesel consumption from 3 different vendors. Consider consolidating to a single diesel supplier for better rates.
Salt Consumption Doubled (+100%)
4 refills in Q1 vs 2 in Q4 at ₹15K each. This could indicate higher water hardness or increased water processing. Worth investigating if softener efficiency has dropped.
Lift Costs Dropped 35%
₹66.3K → ₹43.2K. Q4 included ₹23K in spare parts that didn't recur. The quarterly maintenance contract of ₹43.2K (₹14.4K/month) appears reasonable.
Plumbing is a Recurring Drain
₹2.39L over 6 months across material purchases and labor. December alone saw ₹62.7K in plumbing material. Consider a preventive maintenance program to reduce reactive plumbing costs.
Receipts Growing Fast (+100%)
₹10.4K → ₹20.9K. ParkPlus sticker sales surged in March. Expanding revenue sources (hall rentals, advertising, etc.) could help offset common area expenses.
Capital Investments Needed
STP rework (₹1L), dustbins (₹51.5K), CCTV (₹15K), and other infrastructure items suggest the building needs ongoing capital investment. Budget ₹50K-₹1L/quarter for such items.
